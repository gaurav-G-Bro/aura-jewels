Timeless elegance, modern styling

Aarunya Jewels is a frontend-only demo brand created to show how a premium Indian fashion jewellery store can feel without a backend. The experience includes visual categories, occasion-based shopping, product filters, cart, quick view, wishlist, checkout preview and order tracking demo.
